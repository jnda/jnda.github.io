Business Operating Permit

GENERAL INFORMATION

It is expected that every business within the Jaman North District Assemblyâ€™s jurisdiction obtains a Business Operation Permit.


As the name sounds, a Business operating permit is a license that enables you to undertake/do business in the district.


The Revenue Unit of the District Assembly is in charge of the issuance of BOP. Our hours of operation are Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 5PM. For more information do contact the revenue office of the municipal assembly.


STEPS TO OBTAIN A BUSINESS OPERATING PERMIT

  1. Submission of an application or introductory letter to the District Chief Executive.
  2. Submit relevant documents for processing. The relevant documents include;
    • Registration/Certificate of Incorporation from the Registrar General's Department.
    • Tax Identification Number (TIN).
    • Name of Business.
    • Name of Business Owner.
    • Business Location (GPS location)/Address.
    • Contact Details.
  3. A premises check would be rendered by designated officers of the District Assembly.
